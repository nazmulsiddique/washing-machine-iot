HOW TO AVOID FELTING

HOW TO AVOID FELTING

The fear of felting, tightening or ruining the fibers is always lurking. That's to prevent felting, it is important to remember to avoid thermal shock; use specific detergents for wool and use fabric softeners, to feed the yarn fibers. For example, if you accidentally washed your favorite wool sweater in the washing machine at a fairly high temperature, it will definitely be felted. To fix it, fill a bowl with cold water and add three tablespoons of pure ammonia. Immerse your sweater and let it soak for no more than two hours. After the required time, thoroughly wash it.

HOW TO REMOVE THE DOTS

The unsightly dots can be removed with a razor blade or a razor passed lightly on wool, so the boss will seem new. This trick also works on jackets and coats with inner cuffs or faux fur. In fact, if worn for a long time, the garments lose their soft and silky appearance and sometimes they form clots where the hair is ruined.
HOW TO HANG THE WOOL

HOW TO HANG THE WOOL

It is important never to wring wool or delicate garments in order not to damage the fibers, while it is preferable to dab the excess water with a towel, better if it is neutral in color and with a good absorption capacity. Stretch the sweater on the towel and roll it gently pressing to remove the water. Remember that the sweater should not be stretched vertically but always horizontally, so as not to stretch the fibers and find the pullover along the double! Stretch the garment on a horizontal surface, preferably on a clean towel, to avoid becoming deformed. Or use a drying rack from the bath to get a full ventilation of the head and quicker drying.
IRON THE WOOL

IRON THE WOOL

It is difficult for heavy, thick jerseys to rub around during a trip, so you could also wear them without ironing. The decision to iron or not the head depends on personal preferences. The important thing is to set the iron on the wool or average symbol as if too much heat can cause discoloration or at worst leave burn marks. Stretch the overturned garment and overlay it with a damp cloth. Use the steam function or spray a small amount of water to facilitate the removal of the folds. After ironing, replace the straight shirt, place it on a smooth surface for about 5 minutes to recondition it before folding it. This procedure prevents the formation of folds.
